About

Hi there!

I am a fourth year student at the University of Colorado Boulder pursuing my degree in Marketing with a minor in Creative Technology Design. I am also in the process of gaining certificates in Leadership and Social Responsibility and Ethics (SRE). I am passionate about anything that brings out my artistic side and I seek to promote inclusion in the workplace throughout my career path.

When I'm not at school or work, you can usually find me on the pickleball court!

I have a huge passion for exploring new cultures, I've had the opportunity to immerse myself in diverse experiences and see some incredible sights in both Europe and Asia! I even dedicated some time to learning Japanese.

Outside of my adventures, I'm deeply involved in making a difference in my community. Whether it's through my role on the advisory board for Best Buddies or my previous work as a registered behavioral technician, I'm committed to encouraging inclusivity wherever I can.

Balancing academics and work on my own throughout college has been a journey, but it's one I've navigated successfully. I've landed on the dean's list and participated in programs like Diverse Scholars and the Professional Mentorship program at CU.

I'm incredibly passionate about all things creative in the marketing world—from logos to full-blown brands—and I hope to create a lasting legacy in my career.
